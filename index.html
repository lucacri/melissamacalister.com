Who I Am

I'm a Maine native, transplanted to NYC where I live, work, and play.

I'm the kind of person who arrives at my gate at the airport an hour early because I can't stand being late. In grade school, I was the annoying kid in class who always had her hand raised with the answer and always asked questions. I live by my Google calendar and find deep satisfaction in checking items off my Evernote checklists.

I started my career in corporate ad sales, but for the past several years have worked at high-growth tech startups, where I've helped create systems and processes that instill a method into the madness. I do my best work when I can function cross-departmentally, communicating across business groups to keep everyone marching to the beat of the same drum and generally making sure important stuff gets done.

In my spare time, I enjoy a good barre or yoga class, and am an avid reader (I read 100 books in 2016!). On a Sunday afternoon, you can find me in Central Park with my Yorkie and Maltese pups, and then ordering takeout with my husband. This is New York - who cooks??
