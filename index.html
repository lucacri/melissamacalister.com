Hi there! I'm Mel, and I specialize in helping high-growth companies create order from chaos.

Who I Am

I'm a Maine native, transplanted to NYC where I live, work, and play.

I'm the kind of person who arrives at my gate at the airport an hour early because I can't stand being late. I tend to make a plan for everything and then at least two or three backups - but have learned to find joy in improvising as well. I live by my Google calendar and find deep satisfaction in checking items off my Evernote checklists.

I started my career in corporate ad sales, but for the past several years have worked at high-growth tech startups, where I've helped create systems and processes that instill a method into the madness. I do my best work when I can function cross-departmentally, communicating across business groups to keep everyone marching to the beat of the same drum and generally making sure important stuff gets done. Throughout my career I've also leaned heavily into strategic business development and partnerships, focusing on creating relationships that drive mutual value vs. trying to force square pegs into round holes.

In my spare time, I enjoy a good barre or yoga class, and am an avid reader (I read 100 books in 2016!). On a Sunday afternoon, you can find me in Central Park with my husband and our Yorkie and Maltese pups, followed by attempting a new recipe I saw on Food Network (with varying degrees of success).

In my professional career, I've been fortunate to work with some of the best.
